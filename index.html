Frequently Asked Question

How do I know the right courses for me ?

We offer a personalized course recommendation feature based on your interests, goals, and current skill level. By answering a few simple questions, we can suggest courses that align with your preferences and learning objectives. Additionally, you can explore our diverse course catalog, read course descriptions, and reviews to find the courses that best suit your needs and aspirations.

Do I get a Certificate for every course I take ?

Yes, you will receive a Certificate of Completion for every course you successfully complete on our website.

Do I get a refund if the course isn't worth my money ?

We offer a money-back guarantee if you are not satisfied with the course's content and believe it doesn't meet your expectations.

Will I lifetime a access to courses I purchase ?

Yes, you will have lifetime access to all the courses you purchase on our website. Enjoy learning at your own pace, forever!

Will you assist me to get my first job ?

While we provide comprehensive courses and resources to enhance your skills and increase your job prospects, we cannot guarantee job placement. However, our courses are designed to equip you with valuable knowledge and skills to boost your chances of landing your first job successfully.

What are your payment option ?

We offer multiple payment options, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods for your convenience.

What are requirements to start ?

To get started, all you need is a device with internet access (such as a computer, tablet, or smartphone) and a willingness to learn! There are no specific prerequisites for most of our courses, making it easy for anyone to begin their learning journey. Simply browse our course catalog, choose the one that interests you, and start learning right away!

Do I get all sourse code from project ?

Yes, you will have access to all the source code from the projects covered in our courses. We believe in providing hands-on learning opportunities, and you'll be able to explore, analyze, and understand the code to deepen your understanding of the subject matter.
